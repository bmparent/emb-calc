## Madeira screen references

Approximate Madeira thread screen colors and public color names are derived from
Madeira USA's public electronic color cards:

- [Polyneon electronic color card](https://www.madeirausa.com/_resources/common/userfiles/file/MADEIRA_POLYNEON.pdf)
- [Classic Rayon electronic color card](https://www.madeirausa.com/_resources/common/userfiles/file/MADEIRA_CLASSIC.pdf)
- [Madeira USA resources](https://www.madeirausa.com/resources/)

Madeira states that electronic colors may not precisely match actual thread.
This app uses the values for approximate ranking only and directs users to a
physical Madeira shade card for production approval. Madeira and its product
names are trademarks of their respective owner. This project is not affiliated
with or endorsed by Madeira.
